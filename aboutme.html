Finding Balance

Beyond the demands of my medical practice, I find immense joy and fulfillment in activities that nurture my mind, body, and spirit. Connecting with my Christian faith provides a sense of grounding and purpose, guiding my interactions with patients and colleagues and enriching my personal life. Immersing myself in a good book offers a welcome escape from the daily routine, nourishes my intellectual curiosity, and broadens my perspectives. Expressing my creativity through cooking, particularly exploring the rich flavors and traditions of Pakistani cuisine, brings me immense joy and allows me to share cultural experiences with others, often with my husband, who is a supportive partner and fellow food enthusiast.

  • My faith is an integral part of my life, providing a foundation for my values and a source of strength and guidance in my personal and professional endeavors. It inspires me to approach each patient with compassion, empathy, and a genuine desire to serve.
  • Reading provides a welcome respite from the demands of medicine, allowing me to explore new worlds, engage with diverse perspectives, and cultivate a lifelong love of learning.
  • Cooking, especially exploring the vibrant flavors and culinary traditions of Pakistani cuisine, is a creative outlet that brings me joy and allows me to connect with my cultural heritage. Sharing meals with friends and family creates cherished memories and fosters a sense of community.
